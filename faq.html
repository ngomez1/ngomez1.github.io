Ceremony & Reception

What time should I arrive for the ceremony?

We recommend arriving 30 minutes before the ceremony start time to allow time for parking, finding your seat, and greeting other guests. The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:30 PM.

How long will the ceremony and reception last?

The ceremony will last approximately 30 minutes. Cocktail hour will follow from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM, and the reception is scheduled to end at midnight. You're welcome to stay for as long or as little as you'd like!

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be held indoors in the glasshouse. The cocktail hour will held in the sky garden and art gallery, and the reception will be in the glasshouse. In case of inclement weather, the cocktail hour will be held exclusively in the art gallery.

Travel & Accommodations

Where should I stay?

Please see the Travel page for accommodation suggestions. We recommend booking your accommodations early, as June is a popular time to visit Chicago.

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, complimentary parking is available at Greenhouse Loft. See the Travel page for details.

Will there be transportation provided?

No, but the venue is easily accessible via Interstate 90 and the CTA Blue Line.

Which airport should I fly into?

The nearest airport is O'Hare International Airport (ORD), which is approximately 12 miles from the venue. Most major airlines serve ORD, and rental cars, taxis, and rideshare services are readily available at the airport. Midway International Airport (MDW) is another nearby option.

Attire & Gifts

What is the dress code?

The dress code for our wedding is semi-formal. Men may wear suits, and women may wear cocktail dresses or gowns. Please note that the cocktail hour will be outdoors (weather permitting), so consider appropriate footwear for garden terrain.

Are there any colors I should avoid wearing?

We kindly ask that guests avoid wearing all white, cream, or ivory, as these colors are reserved for the bride. Otherwise, feel free to wear any color you like!

Other Questions

Can I bring a date/plus one?

Due to venue capacity limitations, we can only accommodate those guests named on your invitation. If your invitation includes "and Guest" or your partner's name, you are welcome to bring them. If you have any questions about this, please contact us directly.

Are children invited?

We love your little ones, but we've decided to keep our wedding an adults-only celebration. We are grateful for your understanding.

Will the wedding be photographed/videoed?

Yes, we will have professional photographers capturing our special day. We kindly ask that guests refrain from taking photos during the ceremony, as we'd love to see your faces rather than your phones! You're welcome to take photos during the reception, and we encourage you to share them using our wedding hashtag #LaurenAndNicholas2026.

What if I have food allergies or other dietary restrictions?

We're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please indicate any allergies or dietary needs in your RSVP, and we'll work with our caterer to ensure you have an enjoyable meal.
