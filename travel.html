Travel & Accommodations

Everything you need to know about getting to our wedding and where to stay.

Venue

  • Greenhouse Loft
    2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago, IL 60647
  • About the Venue
    Greenhouse Loft is a beautiful venue featuring a lush sky garden, a stunning glasshouse, and an art gallery. The venue offers both indoor and outdoor spaces, ensuring our celebration will be beautiful regardless of weather. It is located on the second floor of Green Exchange, a rescued historic factory that today is a first-of-its-kind sustainable development project, a designated Chicago Landmark, and a listed property on the National Register of Historic Places.
  • Parking
    Complimentary parking is available on-site at the lot on the SE corner of N Maple Ave and W Diversey Ave. Look for signs that read "Greenhouse Loft Event Parking."
  • Directions
    From O'Hare, take I-90 E for twelve miles to Exit 46A for California Ave. Turn right onto N California Ave, immediately turn left onto W Diversey Ave, and continue for 0.3 miles. Greenhouse Loft (Green Exchange) will be on your right.

Transportation

  • Airport Information
    The nearest airport is O'Hare International Airport (ORD), approximately 12 miles from the venue. Taxis, rideshares, and rental cars are available at the airport. Midway International Airport (MDW) is another option close to downtown Chicago.
  • Rental Cars
    All major rental car companies are available at O'Hare and Midway International Airports. We recommend reserving in advance, especially during the summer season.
  • Public Transportation
    Chicago has excellent public transportation. The CTA Blue Line provides direct access to the venue from O'Hare and from downtown via the Logan Square and California stations, both of which are a 20 minute walk from the venue.

Local Travel Tips

Weather
Chicago in June is typically warm and sunny with average temperatures between 65°F and 85°F. Evenings can be cooler, so consider bringing a light jacket for when the sun goes down.
Local Attractions
Consider enriching your stay by exploring Chicago's famous food scene in the Fulton Market District, beautiful greenery at the Chicago Botanic Garden, and world-class museums in the Loop. The Art Institute, Logan Square Farmers Market, and Lincoln Park Zoo are must see attractions within a few miles of our venue.
Ridesharing
Uber and Lyft are widely available in Chicago and are a convenient option for getting around the city, especially when used in conjunction with the public rail and bus services provided by the CTA.
Parking
Downtown Chicago parking can be limited and expensive. If staying downtown, consider using public transportation or ridesharing services rather than renting a car.
