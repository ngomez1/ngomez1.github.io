Wedding Menu

We're excited to present a handpicked menu of delicious new American fare prepared and served by Lula Cafe, a cherished neighborhood institution and iconic farm-to-table staple of the Logan Square neighborhood. Lula Cafe is a 2024 James Beard Award Winner for outstanding hospitality and a 2020 Michelin Bib Gourmand winner. The restaurant has also been a finalist multiple times for the James Beard Award for Best Chef (Great Lakes). Please see the table below for the complete food menu.

Lula Cafe will also be providing all libations throughout the evening at our open bars, including red, white, and sparkling wine, craft beers, signature and classic cocktails, sodas, sparkling waters, and coffee.
