Wedding Schedule

We're excited to celebrate our special day with you. Here's what to expect during our wedding weekend.

Friday, June 12, 2026 • 6:00 PM

Rehearsal Dinner

TBD Restaurant • 123 TBD Street, Chicago

Join us for a relaxed evening with close family and wedding party members as we prepare for the big day. Dinner and drinks will be provided.

Saturday, June 13, 2026 • 12:00 PM

Wedding Party Preparation

Greenhouse Loft Bridal Suite • 2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago

The wedding party will gather to prepare for the ceremony. Hair and makeup services will be provided for the bridal party.

Saturday, June 13, 2026 • 5:00 PM

Guest Arrival

Greenhouse Loft • 2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago

Please arrive between 5:00pm and 5:15pm to be seated prior to the ceremony.

Saturday, June 13, 2026 • 5:30 PM

Wedding Ceremony

Greenhouse Loft • 2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago

The ceremony will last approximately 30 minutes as we exchange vows in an indoor ceremony space.

Saturday, June 13, 2026 • 6:00 PM

Cocktail Hour

Greenhouse Loft • 2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago

Enjoy drinks and hors d'oeuvres prepared by Lula Cafe while the wedding party takes photos. Space and bar service will be available both indoors and outdoors.

Saturday, June 13, 2026 • 7:00 PM

Reception Dinner & Dancing

Greenhouse Loft • 2545 W Diversey Ave, Chicago

Please join us after the cocktail hour for delicious new American fare prepared and served by Lula Cafe, a cherished neighborhood institution and iconic farm-to-table staple of the Logan Square neighborhood. Lula Cafe is a 2024 James Beard Award Winner for outstanding hospitality and a 2020 Michelin Bib Gourmand winner. The restaurant has also been a finalist multiple times for the James Beard Award for Best Chef (Great Lakes). A night of celebration and dancing to follow dinner.

Additional Information

Dress code for the ceremony and reception is semi-formal attire. The ceremony and all events except Rehearsal Dinner will take place at Greenhouse Loft, which offers both indoor and outdoor spaces. In case of inclement weather, all activities will be moved indoors.
